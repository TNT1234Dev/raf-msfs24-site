About the RAF MSFS 24 Community

We are a community of Microsoft Flight Simulator pilots focused on RAF-themed operations, formation flying, and training. We run regular events, training sessions, and community flyouts. Join the Discord to participate in events and meet other pilots.

What we do

  • Formation and RNAV training
  • Community flyouts and themed events
  • Instructor-led training sessions
